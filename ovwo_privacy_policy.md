# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: June 17, 2026**

Welcome to **ovwo** ("we", "our", or "us"), a private, offline-first period and fertility tracking mobile application. We are committed to protecting your personal and health data. 

This Privacy Policy explains how your information is collected, used, and safegued. Because ovwo is designed with an **offline-first** approach, you have complete control over your data.

---

## 1. Core Privacy Philosophy: Offline-First
We believe your health data is yours alone. 
* **Local Storage**: By default, all data you log in ovwo (including period dates, symptoms, moods, physical metrics, notes, and cycle history) is stored **locally on your device** in a secure SQLite database. 
* **No Access**: We cannot view, access, collect, or sell your health data. It remains on your device unless you explicitly opt-in to cloud features.

---

## 2. Information We Collect (Optional & Premium)
If you choose to use certain features, some data may be processed as follows:

### A. Cloud Sync (Optional Premium Feature)
* If you subscribe to **ovwo Premium** and explicitly create an account to enable Cloud Sync:
  * **Authentication**: We use Firebase Authentication to secure your account (supporting Google Sign-In or Apple Sign-In).
  * **Database Sync**: Your local logs, cycles, and settings are backed up to secure Firestore database servers so you can sync across devices.
  * **Access Control**: This data is fully encrypted in transit and at rest and is accessible only to you through your authenticated account.

### B. Device & OS Security (Local Auth)
* If you enable **App PIN or Biometric Lock** (Face ID / Fingerprint):
  * All authentication is handled directly by your device's operating system security module.
  * ovwo **never** accesses, stores, or transmits your biometric data or passcodes.

### C. RevenueCat / Purchases
* When you purchase a subscription:
  * We use **RevenueCat** to verify and manage your subscription state. 
  * Transactions are processed securely through the Apple App Store or Google Play Store. We do not store credit card or payment credentials.

---

## 3. Data Control, Portability & Deletion
* **Local Backups**: You can export your entire calendar history as a local JSON backup at any time.
* **Data Deletion**: 
  * If you use ovwo offline, deleting the app from your device permanently deletes all local data.
  * If you have active Cloud Sync enabled, you can delete your account and all associated cloud sync data directly through the Profile settings screen.

---

## 4. No Third-Party Tracking or Ads
* We do not display third-party advertisements.
* We do not sell or share your health, period, or symptom logs with insurance companies, advertising networks, or any third-party brokers.
