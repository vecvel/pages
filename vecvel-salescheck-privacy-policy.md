  # Privacy Policy

  Effective Date: April 15, 2026

  Salescheck ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how your information is collected, used, and
  safeguarded when you use the Salescheck mobile application.

  1. Data Ownership and Storage
  We believe that your business data belongs to you. 
   * Local Storage: All sales records, customer details, and financial data you enter into Salescheck are stored locally on your device. We do not have access to
     this data.
   * No Central Server: Salescheck does not transmit your business data to any central servers owned by us. Your financial information remains private to you.

  2. Information We Collect
  We do not collect any personally identifiable information (PII) or financial data from you. The only information processed is that which is necessary for the
  app to function:

   * User-Provided Information: Any data you enter (Product names, prices, customer phone numbers, images) is kept on your device.
   * Images: If you attach product or receipt images, they are stored in your device's local storage.
   * Device Permissions: The app may request access to:
       * Camera/Gallery: To allow you to attach images to sales records.
       * Contacts (Optional): To help you quickly add customer details.
       * Storage: To save and export PDF or CSV reports.

  3. Third-Party Services (Google Drive Sync)
  Salescheck offers an optional feature to back up your data to your personal Google Drive account. 
   * Access: If you enable this feature, the app will ask for permission to access its own hidden folder on your Google Drive (appDataFolder). 
   * Privacy: We cannot see, read, or access any other files on your Google Drive. We do not see your Google account credentials.
   * Policy: Interactions with Google Drive are governed by Google’s Privacy Policy (https://policies.google.com/privacy).

  4. PDF and CSV Exports
  When you generate a PDF or CSV report, the file is created locally on your device. You are responsible for the security of these files when you share them via
  third-party apps (like WhatsApp, Email, or Telegram).

  5. Security
  We implement industry-standard security practices within the app to protect your data. However, the security of your data also depends on your device's
  security (passcodes, biometrics). We recommend keeping your device updated and secured.

  6. Children’s Privacy
  Salescheck is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

  7. Changes to This Policy
  We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by the "Effective Date" at the top of this document. We encourage you to
  review this policy periodically.
