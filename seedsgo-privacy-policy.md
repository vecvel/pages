# Privacy Policy for Seedsgo

**Last Updated: June 12, 2026**

Vecvel ("we," "our," or "us") operates the Seedsgo mobile application (the "App"). We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information in connection with your use of the App.

By downloading, installing, or using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

---

## 1. Information Collection and Use

### Local Device Storage
Seedsgo stores your gameplay data, leaf balances, settings, and player preferences directly and securely on your device using Flutter Secure Storage (which utilizes iOS Keychain and Android KeyStore). This data remains strictly on your local device and is not transmitted to or stored on our servers.

### Personally Identifiable Information (PII)
We do not directly collect, store, or share any personally identifiable information (such as your name, address, email address, or phone number) through the App.

---

## 2. Third-Party Services
To improve performance, resolve bugs, and support development, the App utilizes third-party services. These services may collect information used to identify you or your device.

The privacy policies of the third-party providers used by the App can be reviewed below:

- **Google Play Services**: [Google Play Services Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)
- **Firebase Crashlytics** (Diagnostic crash reporting): [Firebase Privacy Policy](https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy)
- **Google Mobile Ads (AdMob)** (Ad serving and personalization): [Google Privacy & Terms](https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads)

These third-party providers may collect details such as your device's Internet Protocol ("IP") address, device name, operating system version, app configuration during crashes, and advertising identifier (IDFA / AAID) for ad personalization.

## 3. Children's Privacy
These Services do not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13 years of age. If we discover that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we immediately delete this from our servers (if applicable). If you are a parent or guardian and you are aware that your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so that we can take the necessary actions.
